Why Private AI?

Modern AI models like ChatGPT have hundreds of billions of parameters and require highly specialized hardware to run at useful speeds. That's why most companies rely on cloud providers. But when you use AI online, a key question always comes up:

What happens to your data?

Most business processes involve sensitive or even regulated data. Simply putting that into a public AI service is rarely an option. That's where our Private AI Solutions come in: secure, tailored setups that let you use AI with full confidence.

1. Cloud Solution – Scalable & Always Up to Date

Our cloud solution is the best choice if you want scalability and the very latest AI capabilities.

EU & Swiss Compliance

Runs on EU or Swiss servers, ensuring local data residency and compliance.

Logically Private Instance

Your data is isolated from other users. Microsoft guarantees: data is used only to deliver the service, never stored or reused.

Latest AI Models

Always access the latest models, with no hardware limitations. Perfect for teams with many users, heavy workloads, or fast-changing AI needs.

Best for: Companies that want maximum performance, scalability, and regulatory compliance — with strong guarantees on privacy.

2. Local Solution – Ultimate Control

If your top concern is absolute control of your data, we also provide a fully local setup.

Specialized Hardware

Delivered with a specialized rack system we designed to optimize for cost and AI performance.

Open-Source Models

Runs powerful open-source AI models at usable speeds without requiring million-dollar hardware.

Complete Control

Tailored to your business needs: we adjust model size and hardware configuration depending on your output requirements. Your data never leaves your premises — you're in full control.

Best for: Organizations with highly confidential data or strict regulatory environments that do not allow cloud usage.
