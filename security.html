Your Data, Your Choice

Every organization has different security needs and infrastructure. We offer flexible deployment options that put you in control of your data while delivering powerful AI capabilities.

Choose the approach that fits your business: fully managed Swiss cloud, hybrid deployment that keeps your data on-premise, or let's work together to design a custom solution that meets your specific requirements. We're committed to finding the right approach for your organization.

Swiss Private Cloud

Fully managed, dedicated infrastructure in Switzerland. No hardware investment, no operational overhead.

Dedicated Environment

Your own private cloud instance that never shares resources with other customers. Complete isolation for maximum security.

Swiss Data Residency

All data stays in Switzerland. No cross-border transfers, full compliance with Swiss data protection laws.

Fully Managed

We handle all infrastructure, security updates, and monitoring. You focus on your AI applications, we handle the rest.

Hybrid On-Premise

Keep your data on-premise while accessing powerful AI processing through secure connections.

Data Stays Local

Secure AI Processing

Best of Both Worlds
