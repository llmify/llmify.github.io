Our Approach

We believe when it comes to building successful integrations of agents, there are a few key things to consider.

Intuitive Integration

We leverage LLM capabilities to make usage as intuitive as possible – it should feel like onboarding another employee. Our agents integrate seamlessly via standard business communication channels like email or other platforms, augmented with our Agent Cockpit that gives you a unique view of what the agent is working on, their current backlog, and well-thought-through visualizations of results. This allows co-workers to easily confirm results, building trust while making everything validatable and decisions traceable.

Reliability Over Flash

Rather than building agents that deliver astonishing results 50% of the time like many fully autonomous agent systems advertise, we believe there's great value in carefully designed systems. We leverage LLMs when confronted with uncertain structures, using them systematically to restructure data, then perform transformations deterministically. This approach improves overall system reliability and explainability – crucial properties for highly regulated tasks.

Seamless Workflow Integration

Consistent Reliability

Delivers predictable, high-quality results through our hybrid approach of intelligent data restructuring and deterministic processing

Max – The Onboarding Agent

Max takes care of client onboarding in regulated environments. From verifying KYC documents for individuals and legal entities to ensuring compliance checks, Max relieves your legal and compliance teams of repetitive work. He integrates smoothly into your workflow, just like another team member.

Clara – The Auditing Agent

Clara automates balance sheet reviews and document preparation. Instead of manually transferring financial statements into spreadsheets and running validations, Clara takes over these steps with precision and speed. She supports your audit team in ensuring accuracy while saving valuable hours.
