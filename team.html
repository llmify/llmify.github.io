Our Journey to LLMify

From regulatory compliance experts to AI automation pioneers â€“ here's how we discovered the power of intelligent process automation.

1

Z22 Spin-off Foundations

Started as a small core team with FINMA licence expertise, specializing in developing and deploying software in highly regulated environments. We understood the complexities of compliance from day one.

2

The Documentation Challenge

New regulations brought extensive documentation requirements and monitoring. To avoid drowning in paperwork, we built deterministic automation first, then leveraged LLMs like ChatGPT to automate increasingly complex tasks.

3

Client Interest & Validation

After sharing our automation successes with clients, we received overwhelmingly positive feedback. They expressed strong interest in applying our proven processes to automate their own workflows.

4

LLMify AG is Born

Founded LLMify AG as a separate entity to build a dedicated team around sharing the knowledge and lessons learned over years of automation experience, helping other companies optimize their processes.

The Result

Today, we combine deep regulatory expertise with cutting-edge AI automation to help businesses transform their most complex, document-heavy processes into streamlined, intelligent workflows.
